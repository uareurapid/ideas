## General Ideas (5)

**Idea 1: GiftMemory**
People repeatedly give bad gifts because they forget what the recipient already has or mentioned wanting. GiftMemory lets you log people in your life, track their interests, wishlist hints from conversations, and get timely reminders before birthdays/holidays — all in a simple mobile app.

**Idea 2: WaitlistPerks**
Businesses lose customers who hit long queues and leave. WaitlistPerks is a simple SaaS for local businesses (restaurants, clinics, salons) to manage digital queues and automatically send a small discount or perk to customers who wait — turning frustration into loyalty.

**Idea 3: HabitSplit**
Couples and roommates often fail at shared goals (saving money, eating healthier, going to the gym) because there's no accountability layer between them. HabitSplit is a two-player habit tracker where both people commit to a shared goal and get nudged together — social pressure without a public audience.

**Idea 4: TripCalc**
Group trips always turn into financial chaos — who paid what, who owes whom, which expenses are shared vs. personal. TripCalc is a dead-simple mobile app specifically for trip expense splitting, with a trip timeline view and one-tap settlement at the end.

**Idea 5: SkillProof**
Freelancers and job seekers struggle to show *verifiable* skills beyond a resume. SkillProof lets professionals complete short real-world micro-tasks in their field (design a logo, write a SQL query, fix a bug) and get a shareable proof-of-skill badge that links back to their actual work output.

## Developer Tools (3)

**Idea 6: PricingLab 🛠️ DEV TOOL**
Indie hackers and SaaS founders constantly guess at pricing, often undercharging or structuring tiers poorly. PricingLab is a tool that lets you run A/B pricing experiments on your landing page without engineering effort — swap prices, test anchoring, and see which tiers convert, all behind a simple script tag.

**Idea 7: OpenGraphKit 🛠️ DEV TOOL**
Most developers ship apps with generic or broken Open Graph previews, killing click-through rates on social shares. OpenGraphKit is a SaaS that generates dynamic, branded OG images from a URL pattern — auto-pulling your page title, description, and branding — with a one-line integration and a live preview tester.

**Idea 8: BetaCircle 🛠️ DEV TOOL**
Getting quality beta testers is painful — Twitter callouts attract tire-kickers and Product Hunt is crowded. BetaCircle is a curated platform where indie developers post their apps for structured beta testing, testers earn reputation scores based on feedback quality, and founders get actionable reports instead of empty signups.
