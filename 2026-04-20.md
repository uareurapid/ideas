## General Ideas (5)

**Idea 1: SleepDebt**
Most people know they're tired but have no sense of how much sleep debt they've actually accumulated. SleepDebt tracks your cumulative deficit over weeks, shows how it maps to your mood/focus patterns, and gives you a realistic payoff plan — not just "sleep 8 hours tonight."

**Idea 2: ShopLocal Map**
Tourists and even locals rarely know which cafés, bookshops, or markets are independent vs. chain-owned. ShopLocal overlays a city map with crowd-sourced "indie verified" tags so you can consciously choose to spend your money in the local economy rather than corporate chains.

**Idea 3: AllergyCard**
Travelers and people with food allergies struggle to communicate their restrictions abroad or in unfamiliar restaurants. AllergyCard generates a printable/shareable card with your specific allergies translated into 30+ languages — handy for restaurants, street food, and travel abroad.

**Idea 4: ChoreWar**
Household chore assignments cause real friction in shared apartments and families. ChoreWar turns chores into a lightweight competitive game with a fair rotation engine, points, and a weekly leaderboard — making accountability feel less passive-aggressive and more playful.

**Idea 5: PackRight**
Packing for trips is stressful and people consistently forget things. PackRight learns from your past trips (destination, duration, activities) and generates a smart packing list that improves over time — flagging things you forgot last time and adapting for weather forecasts.

## Developer Tools (3)

**Idea 6: EarlySignal 🛠️ DEV TOOL**
Indie developers launch products but have no visibility into whether their landing page copy is actually converting or just generating bounces. EarlySignal is a lightweight heatmap + scroll-depth + CTA analytics tool specifically designed for solo devs and tiny teams — zero-config embed, free tier that actually works, no enterprise fluff.

**Idea 7: DemoFlow 🛠️ DEV TOOL**
Getting a prospect to "get it" quickly is the hardest part of selling a dev tool or SaaS. DemoFlow lets you record interactive product walkthroughs (click-through HTML demos, not video) that prospects can actually poke around in — shareable via link, embeddable on landing pages, no screen recording skills required.

**Idea 8: LocaleReady 🛠️ DEV TOOL**
Developers add i18n support late in the process and it becomes a painful refactor. LocaleReady is a CLI + CI tool that scans your codebase for hardcoded strings, flags missing translation keys, and auto-generates locale file scaffolding — catching localization debt before it explodes at scale.
