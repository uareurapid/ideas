**Idea 1: LocalSkillSwap**

**Idea 2: MealPrepBuddy**
Smart meal prep assistant that learns your dietary preferences, grocery shopping patterns, and kitchen inventory. Auto-generates weekly meal plans optimized for minimal waste, batch cooking efficiency, and reusing ingredients. Integrates with grocery delivery APIs for one-click ordering of only what you need.

**Idea 3: FocusRent**
Rent your focus to others for accountability. Need to write a report? Match with someone who needs to study. You both video call and work silently together for 25-90 min sessions. Built-in Pomodoro, ambient sounds, and "focus streaks" that unlock perks. Targets remote workers and students struggling with discipline.

**Idea 4: SubscriptionSniper**
Analyzes your bank statements and credit cards to find forgotten subscriptions, then auto-negotiates cancellations or better rates. One-time fee or percentage of money saved. Focuses on "subscription creep" - those $9.99/month services you signed up for 2 years ago and forgot.

**Idea 5: PetSitterSwap**
Community-based pet sitting exchange for neighbors with pets. You watch my dog this weekend, I'll watch yours next month. Credit-based system, emergency backup sitters, vet info storage, and real-time pet cam sharing. Saves hundreds on traditional pet boarding.
