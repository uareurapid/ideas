## General Ideas (5)

**Idea 1: Micro-Commitment Planner**
Most people fail at goals because they're too big. An app that breaks down any goal into 5-minute daily actions you can't say no to. Track streaks, celebrate tiny wins, and gradually increase commitment as habits form.

**Idea 2: Local Errand Matchmaker**
Connect neighbors who need errands done (grocery pickup, prescription collection, package drops) with people already going that way. Real-time matching based on routes, not destinations. Uber for favors with micro-payments.

**Idea 3: Recipe Rescue**
Snap a photo of ingredients in your fridge, get instant recipes that use exactly what you have before it expires. Tracks expiration dates via receipt scanning and sends "use this tonight" alerts. Reduces food waste while solving "what's for dinner?"

**Idea 4: Focus Timer with Stakes**
Pomodoro timer that locks your phone and charges you money if you unlock early. Set your own penalty ($1-$20), money goes to charity or friends. Gamified with leaderboards for accountability partners.

**Idea 5: Parking Spot Handoff**
People leaving parking spots in busy areas can sell them to people arriving. Real-time GPS matching, 2-minute handoff window. Seller gets $2-5, buyer saves 20 minutes circling. Works for street parking, events, airports.
