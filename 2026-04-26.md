## General Ideas (5)

**Idea 1: DeathReady**
Most people have no record of their funeral wishes, passwords, insurance policies, or who to notify when they die — leaving families scrambling during an already painful moment. A private digital vault where you store final wishes, important documents, digital account credentials, and a contact list that can be unlocked by a trusted person when the time comes. Deeply underserved niche (taboo = low competition, high willingness to pay).

**Idea 2: ConferenceMatch**
Conferences cost thousands to attend but networking is mostly random hallway collisions — there's no way to find the right people before or during the event. An app where attendees of the same event discover each other by role/interest, request 15-minute coffee chats, and share session notes, all within a temporary event-scoped community. Like Bumble BFF meets professional networking, without the awkward LinkedIn cold DM.

**Idea 3: GardenGuide**
Beginner gardeners routinely plant at the wrong time, space plants incorrectly, and lose crops to preventable issues — wasting money on seeds and hours of effort. Enter your zip code and what you want to grow; the app builds a personalized planting calendar with frost date alerts, companion planting suggestions, watering reminders, and a harvest log. Taps into the massive post-pandemic home gardening boom that has no great dedicated app.

**Idea 4: BrewLog**
Home brewers of beer, wine, kombucha, and cider juggle recipes, fermentation timers, gravity readings, and tasting notes across scattered notebooks and spreadsheets. A clean mobile brewing logbook with recipe builder, fermentation stage tracker, batch history, and tasting notes — purpose-built for the millions in the growing homebrew community. Simple enough for beginners, detailed enough for serious hobbyists.

**Idea 5: ApplicantTrack**
Active job seekers apply to dozens of companies and lose track of where they are, what stage they're in, and when to follow up — typically resorting to messy spreadsheets that fall apart within a week. A personal CRM for the job hunt: log applications, set automated follow-up reminders, track interview rounds, and compare offers side-by-side. Like Trello but designed specifically for the modern job search.
