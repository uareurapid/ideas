## General Ideas (5)

**Idea 1: Clarity Coach**
Most productivity apps track tasks, but people struggle with *knowing what to work on*. An AI-powered daily planning app that asks you context-rich questions each morning ("What's draining your energy right now?", "What outcome matters most this week?") and converts messy thoughts into a prioritized 3-task focus list. No overwhelming boards or hundred-item backlogs.

**Idea 2: RetentionKit for AI Apps**
AI apps are seeing terrible 30-day retention (industry reports show drops of 60-80%). A specialized analytics + engagement toolkit that tracks *why* users leave AI products specifically: prompt fatigue, unclear value, hallucination frustration. Includes playbooks for re-engagement campaigns tailored to AI product patterns.

**Idea 3: BrainDump Pro**
Existing note apps are too structured for ADHD/divergent thinkers. An app that lets you voice-dump or type-stream thoughts with zero organization required, then uses AI to auto-cluster related ideas, surface patterns, and gently suggest "you mentioned this 4 times — maybe it matters?" No folders, no tags you have to maintain, just intelligent emergence from chaos.

**Idea 4: AEO Dashboard (Agentic Engine Optimization)**
Google search is dying; AI agents (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) are the new discovery layer. Brands have no visibility into whether AI systems mention them. An SEO-style dashboard that monitors how often your brand appears in AI agent responses, tracks citation quality, and suggests content optimizations to rank in "agentic search" results.

**Idea 5: Walk-to-Unlock**
Screen time apps fail because willpower fails. A phone locker that requires you to walk a set distance (tracked via pedometer) before unlocking your phone. Perfect for breaking doomscroll cycles. Can set different unlock requirements per app (100 steps for Instagram, 50 for messages). Gamifies getting off the couch.
