**Idea 1: BrainDump Voice**
An ADHD-friendly app that lets you speak all your scattered thoughts, tasks, and ideas into your phone throughout the day. AI automatically categorizes them into todos, project notes, reminders, and ideas without you having to organize anything. Voice-first capture beats typing when your brain is moving fast.

**Idea 2: Neighborhood Skill Swap**
A hyper-local app (radius-based) where neighbors exchange skills instead of money. Need your bike fixed? Swap 1 hour of bike repair for 1 hour of your graphic design skills. Built-in reputation system prevents abuse, focuses on building real community connections vs cold transactions.

**Idea 3: Meeting Cost Tracker**
A calendar integration that shows the real-time $ cost of every meeting based on attendee salaries/rates. Displays a running counter during calls ("This meeting has cost $487 so far"). Makes companies aware of meeting bloat and helps justify ending calls early when they've gone off track.

**Idea 4: Honest Product Reviews Marketplace**
Users get paid small amounts (crypto or cash) to write detailed, verified purchase reviews. Reviewers must prove purchase (receipt/order ID). Buyers access unfiltered reviews without fake 5-star spam. Revenue from brands paying for verified feedback insights rather than manipulated ratings on Amazon/G2.

Lets non-technical founders launch MVPs in days, not months. Targets the "I have an idea but can't code" crowd who waste money on expensive dev agencies.
